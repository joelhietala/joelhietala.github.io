This website was created as a part of a course by JAMK. The project involved designing and developing an ecommerce website using Laravel/PHP.

My main responsibilty was the front-end and the design of the site. So basically the design in figma and develop it with HTML/CSS and Bootstrap. Other job I had, was to take care of the deployment of the site. We decided to use DigitalOcean as a platform.

As the project progressed, I also gained some experience working with Laravel and PHP.
